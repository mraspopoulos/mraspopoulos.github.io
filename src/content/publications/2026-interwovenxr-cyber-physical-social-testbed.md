Robotics and hyperautomation are moving from specialized tools to core capabilities for designing, operating, and governing complex systems. This article examines the convergence of robotics, digital twins, and artificial intelligence orchestration within cyber–physical–social systems, where robots act as embodied computing nodes across physical and digital worlds, highlighting enabling technologies and adoption pathways for scalable hyperautomation.
