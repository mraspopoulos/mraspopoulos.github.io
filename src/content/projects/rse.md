Responsible research and innovation (RRI) is an approach that assesses potential implications and societal expectations related to research and innovation, aiming to foster the design of ethical, inclusive and sustainable research and innovation. Informed by RRI, the project focuses on empirical data collection to support the development of a set of guidelines for the implementation and use of smart environments that employ the technology of the Internet-of-Things (IoT)
