FUTON aimed to research, develop and validate a flexible architecture for wireless systems based on the joint processing of the radio signals from distinct remote antenna units and supported by a transparent fibre infrastructure. This architecture enables the high bit rates targeted in the broadband component of future wireless systems and provides a framework for the integration of heterogeneous wireless systems
