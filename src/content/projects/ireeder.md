The main objective of the IREEDER project is to improve the capacity of higher education in Jordan, using state of the art technology and training staff on improving the quality of the materials taught by making best use of these technologies. The developed subjects will be oriented towards the recent technologies in electrical engineering including Renewable Energy, Internet of Things and Cybersecurity, in addition to their different applications. All these subjects will be in accordance with EU requirements. Specific objectives are: Develop, integrate, accredit and evaluate subjects with appropriate laboratories' components in the fields of RE, IoT and CS taught by universities in Jordan and brought into line with the EU requirements. These subjects will be developed and taught in English language. Engage faculty members in the development of interactive instruction techniques for lectures, laboratory training, and sharing experiences with EU partner universities. Develop and implement subject content using Virtual Learning Environment (VLE) delivery and remote labs. Extend services and training in collaboration with the industry firms and local communities. Improve the human capacity of Jordanian universities by providing training and upgrading opportunities in the EU for aspiring young and women academic staff
