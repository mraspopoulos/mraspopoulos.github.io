MOBILIA targeted ITU IMT-advanced requirements for future wireless systems, i.e. peak data rates of 100 Mbps for mobile applications and 1 Gbps for low mobility. The IMT-advanced vision of future network as being formed of interworking access systems was also considered. A derived target was to obtain an increased aggregate throughput/user satisfaction vs. existing systems
